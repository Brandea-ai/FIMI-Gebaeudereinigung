# Conversion Psychology & UX Analysis

## 1. The "Lizard Brain" First
Users decide in **0.05 seconds** if they trust a site.
*   **Current Issue:** Generic images (being fixed) and lack of human faces.
*   **Fix:** The new "Authentic" images with Andreas & Maria are critical. Humans trust humans. Seeing a face (especially smiling/eye contact) releases oxytocin (trust hormone).

## 2. Cognitive Biases to Leverage
*   **Authority Bias:** You mention "ISO Standards".
    *   *Optimization:* Don't just write it. Show the certificate icon. Use words like "Audit", "Zertifiziert", "Meisterbetrieb" (if applicable).
*   **Social Proof:** "120+ Kunden".
    *   *Optimization:* Use logos of well-known local companies (even if small). "Known" is better than "Many".
*   **Risk Aversion:** B2B buyers are afraid of making a mistake (hiring a bad cleaner = looking bad to the boss).
    *   *Optimization:* Use "Risk Reversal" language. "Zufriedenheitsgarantie", "Keine Vertragsbindung in den ersten 3 Monaten", "Kostenlose Probereinigung".

## 3. The User Journey (The "Funnel")
1.  **Awareness (SEO):** User lands on `/leistungen/bueroreinigung`.
2.  **Interest (Hero):** "Ah, they do exactly what I need."
3.  **Desire (Benefits):** "They are fast (2h response) and German-quality."
4.  **Action (CTA):** The CTA must be low-friction.
    *   *Bad CTA:* "Registrieren" or "Kunde werden".
    *   *Good CTA:* "Preis anfragen" or "Kostenlos besichtigen".
    *   *Current Form:* The multi-step form is GREAT. It uses the "Sunk Cost Fallacy" – once they start clicking, they are likely to finish.

## 4. Copywriting Tone
*   **Current:** Professional, slightly dry.
*   **Target:** "Competent but Human".
    *   *Avoid:* "Wir bieten Dienstleistungen im Bereich der Gebäudereinigung an." (Passive, boring).
    *   *Use:* "Wir sorgen dafür, dass Ihr Büro glänzt – jeden Morgen." (Active, benefit-driven).

## 5. Color Psychology
*   **Navy (#012956):** Trust, Authority, Stability. (Good for Footer/Headlines).
*   **Teal (#109387):** Cleanliness, Freshness, Modernity. (Good for CTAs/Highlights).
*   **White Space:** Essential for a "Premium" feel. Don't clutter. The current layout is good, but ensure the new images have room to breathe.
